Architectural & Design Services

At TATHA ARCHITECTS, we transform visions into functional art. With 12+ years of expertise in Bangalore and beyond, our services blend innovation, sustainability, and cultural sensitivity.

01.

Residential Architect

We design homes that are a true reflection of your lifestyle and aspirations. Our process involves deep collaboration to create personalized spaces that balance aesthetics with everyday functionality. From modern apartments to sprawling villas, we focus on light, ventilation, and spatial harmony. We manage the entire journey, from initial concept to the final handover of keys. Your dream home is built with precision, transparency, and a deep understanding of your vision.

Landscape Architecture

We extend your living space beyond the walls by designing harmonious outdoor areas. Our landscape designs include gardens, courtyards, terraces, and water features that promote relaxation and connection with nature. Sustainable Architecture

We are committed to creating buildings that minimize environmental impact and maximize energy efficiency. Our sustainable designs incorporate passive solar techniques, rainwater harvesting, renewable materials, and superior insulation. We aim for green building certifications like LEED and IGBC, ensuring your project is healthy for occupants and the planet. This approach results in spaces that are not only eco-conscious but also cost-effective to maintain in the long run.
