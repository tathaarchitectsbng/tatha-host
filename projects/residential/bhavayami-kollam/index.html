    Bhavayami Residence – A 2,400 sq. ft Contemporary Home in Kollam

    The Bhavayami Residence in Kollam is a 2,400 sq ft home completed in 2022, showcasing the contemporary design vision of TATHA ARCHITECTS. Focused on spatial efficiency, the residence integrates natural ventilation and modern comforts to create a healthy living environment. Its design balances simplicity and elegance, providing both functionality and timeless aesthetic appeal. Warm interiors and efficient planning make this residence ideal for modern lifestyles while retaining a homely character. Through this project, TATHA ARCHITECTS demonstrates its expertise in crafting personalized residential spaces across India.
