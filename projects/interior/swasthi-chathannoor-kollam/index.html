    Swasthi Chathannoor, Kollam

    4,500 sq ft Modern Home Interiors (2023), Kollam

    Swasthi Chathannoor, completed in 2023, is a 4,500 sq ft residence located in Kollam. Designed by TATHA ARCHITECTS, the home blends contemporary architectural sensibilities with timeless comfort. Its interiors emphasize natural light, fluid circulation, and the seamless balance between private and social spaces. Every element, from material choices to spatial detailing, enhances both functionality and elegance. The residence stands as a warm family home that combines luxury, practicality, and aesthetics, reaffirming TATHA’s expertise in crafting modern living environments.
