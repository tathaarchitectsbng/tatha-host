    10,600 sq ft Commercial Project in Bengaluru (2023)

    The Bhima Commercial Street project is a landmark development completed in 2023 in the heart of Bengaluru. Spanning 10,600 sq ft, it showcases TATHA ARCHITECTS’ expertise in blending modern functionality with brand-focused retail design. The planning emphasizes smooth customer flow, strong visibility, and an engaging shopping environment. Thoughtful material choices and architectural detailing ensure both durability and aesthetic appeal. This project reinforces TATHA ARCHITECTS as a leading Bangalore-based firm shaping future-ready commercial spaces.
