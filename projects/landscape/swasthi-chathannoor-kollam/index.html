    Swasthi Chathannoor, Kollam

    Chathannoor Residence – Elegant Living in 4,500 sq. ft of Modern Comfort

    Completed in 2023, this 4,500 sq. ft residence in Chathannoor, India, reflects Tatha Architects’ vision of blending contemporary design with timeless comfort. The home is thoughtfully crafted with spacious interiors, natural light integration, and a seamless flow between private and social spaces. Every detail—from material selection to spatial planning—has been designed to enhance functionality while expressing a refined architectural character. This residence stands as a modern yet warm family home that balances luxury, practicality, and aesthetics.
