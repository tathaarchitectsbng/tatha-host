    About Us

    We are a Bangalore-based architectural studio that transforms visions into timeless structures. Blending innovation with tradition, we craft residential, commercial, and sustainable spaces that tell stories.

    TATHA ARCHITECTS LLP

    01.

    Since our inception in 2013, TATHA ARCHITECTS has been dedicated to redefining the architectural landscape of Bangalore and beyond. We believe that exceptional design is a harmonious blend of aesthetic vision, functional clarity, and environmental responsibility. Our philosophy is rooted in the principle that architecture is not merely about constructing buildings, but about crafting experiences and narratives that resonate deeply with people and their environment.

    We offer a comprehensive suite of services, encompassing Residential, Commercial, Tropical, and Sustainable architecture, along with bespoke Interior and Nature-Integrated designs. Our process is collaborative and transparent, leveraging our proprietary BIN project management tool to provide clients with real-time updates and unwavering clarity from concept to completion.

    Our team brings together over 12 years of diverse expertise, successfully delivering more than 100 projects that stand as testaments to our commitment to quality and innovation. We prioritize sustainable practices, employing eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient solutions to minimize our environmental footprint and create future-proof spaces.

    At TATHA ARCHITECTS, we are more than just designers; we are your partners in building a legacy. We strive to transform your vision into built reality, creating spaces that are not only beautiful and functional but also tell your unique story.
